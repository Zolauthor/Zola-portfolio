
Context
Sales analytics refers to the process of extracting valuable insights from sales-related data, metrics, and trends. Its objective is to forecast future sales performance and set targets based on the available information. In contrast, sales analysis involves transforming and analyzing data to evaluate a sales team's performance relative to its goals. This approach provides insights into the performance of individual products or services, highlights areas of difficulty in selling, identifies new market opportunities, forecasts future sales, and identifies sales activities that generate revenue.


Data Fields
Order ID - An Order ID is the number system used to keep track of orders. Each order receives its own Order ID that will not be duplicated.

Product - Refers to the item(s) sold to the customer.

Quantity Ordered - The total quantity of items ordered in the original order, prior to any modifications.

Price Each - The price of each individual product.

Order Date - The date on which the customer requests their order to be shipped.

Purchase Address - The address obtained from the purchase order.
